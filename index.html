About Me

My name is Nathan Goodman, i'm 19 years of age and currently live in Oregon City. I have lived in Oregon all of my life and call it my home. I am huge animal lover with 2 dogs and 2 cats whom I love to take on walks. Technology and music are most definitely my passions in life, whether it's building computers or playing guitar I can't get enough. This portfolio will display my progress throughout this exciting journey and I cannot wait to share this with the world.

Background

I graduated from Oregon City High School in 2018, my original path for education was going to be at Berklee College of Music in Bostson MA. After some reassessing with what I really wanted out of an education, I decided to not go to school for music, and instead see what kind of skills I could gain in the technology world. I've always had a deep interest for computers and can give my thanks to the internet for teaching me skills upon skills. I decided to put a pause on pursuing a degree to try Epicodus, I am hoping this will turn into a valuable and life-changing experience for me.
